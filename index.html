About

The daughter of an artist and a scientist, Haley approaches her craft with studied technicality - yet strives to create work that radiates with life. Haley feels the most at peace when working with traditional media, and embraces the infinite possibilities of the digital world. The melding of old-world and modern aesthetic is what drives her to create relevant work that resonates with humankind.

  • Education and Training

    Haley has pursued a rigorous and technical artistic education her entire life, from specialized courses to an accredited private art school education. During her college career at Rocky Mountain College of Art + Design, she pursued graphic design, art education, and illustration majors; graduating with a BFA in Illustration.

  • Professional Career

    Haley's freelance design and illustration clients range from personal commissions to authors to PR firms to major retail malls.

Rally King Logo Design

Client testimony: "Haley wrapped up our logo, including several final versions, in just three weeks while taking finals.
